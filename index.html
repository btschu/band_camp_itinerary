Expectations

Band Camp is a South High School sanctioned event, therefore students must follow the school’s behavior code and dress code. If any student is suspected and found in possession or using drugs/ alcohol, the local authorities will be notified immediately. It is important to remember that we are representing South High School at all times and appropriate behavior is expected.
